    Manage your guests requirements

    All guests on Airbnb are required to have:

    • Profile Photo
    • Confirmed email
    • Confirmed phone number
    • Payment information

    Before booking your home, each guest must:

    • Agree to your House Rules
    • Send you a message about their trip
    • Tell you how many people are coming
    • Confirm their check-in time if they’re arriving within 2 days

    You need to feel confident with every reservation. That’s why we require certain information from every guest before they can book.
